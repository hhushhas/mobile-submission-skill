# Product Quality For Mobile Growth

Use before recommending paid acquisition or major launch spend. Product quality is a growth lever: weak activation, retention, ratings, performance, or trust will make paid acquisition expensive and fragile.

## Pre-Ads Dashboard

Ask the project to measure or estimate:

- store-page conversion rate
- activation event completion
- D1, D7, D14, and D30 retention
- crash-free sessions/users
- Android ANR/crash rate and core vitals
- app startup time, jank, battery, and app size
- revenue per install or trial-to-paid conversion when monetized
- cancellation reasons and refund signals
- review sentiment and support themes

If these are unknown, recommend a small validation launch before scaling spend.

## Quality Checks

- Onboarding: optimize for first real value, not just account creation.
- Activation: track 1-2 events that predict retention or payment.
- Retention: diagnose D1/D7 drop-off before scaling ads.
- Monetization: validate pricing, trial-to-paid, renewal, and cancellation reasons.
- Review prompts: ask after a win moment; never on first launch.
- Support: make support easy to find and respond to reviews.
- Accessibility: support basic screen reader, contrast, tap target, and text-size expectations.
- Localization: localize app UI, listing, screenshots, and support/privacy pages together.
- App size: keep downloads small; large apps hurt install conversion.
- Permissions: request only core permissions and explain them plainly.
- Store creative: screenshots and ads should match real app value.
